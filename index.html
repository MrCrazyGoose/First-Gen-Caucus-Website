What We Do

Below is a brief overview of our mission, values, and core activities.

  • We amplify the voices of first-gen students at all levels of our organization through petitions, campaigns, and direct advocacy efforts.

  • We provide tools, trainings, and mentorship to help our members become confident leaders in their communities.

  • Our events, workshops, and volunteer initiatives foster connections between first-gen students, alumni, and supportive allies.
